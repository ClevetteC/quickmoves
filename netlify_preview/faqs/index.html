Pricing and estimates

How do you quote a move?+
We do an in-person or video walkthrough, then send a written estimate. Flat rate available on request.

Never. We discuss all fees before, during, and after your job.
Do you offer flat-rate moves?+
Yes. Ask for one at quote time.
Do you require a deposit?+
Call us at (914) 732-3733 for current deposit policy details.

Logistics

What areas do you serve?+
Westchester County, New York City, and Fairfield County, CT. Call (914) 732-3733 if you don't see your town listed. See the areas we serve page.
How far in advance should I book?+
Two to four weeks is ideal in peak season (May through September). We do take last-minute jobs when the schedule allows.
Can you move me on a weekend?+
Yes, with booking lead time.
Can I upload a video for an estimate?+
Yes. Record a walkthrough on your phone and upload it at the video estimate page. Steven or Frankie reviews it personally and sends pricing back the same day during business hours.
